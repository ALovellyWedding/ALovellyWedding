Ceremony

Our wedding ceremony will take place from 6:00pm - 7:00pm at Hope Wesleyan Church in Independence Iowa. Due to the size of the church the ceremony will be for very close family. Those attending the ceremony will receive an invitation specifically calling for their attendance.

Reception

Our reception will officially begin at 8:00pm at the Heartland Acres Event Center in Independence Iowa. However, guests are invited to arrive anytime between 7:00pm and 8:00pm as there will be a Cocktail hour taking place at that time where drinks and snacks will be available. Festivities will begin at 8:00pm and continue on until the New Year arrives. :)

Mooore Reception Details

A full-fledged meal will not be served at the reception so we advise eating something before attending.

Finger food of all kinds will be served in various intervals for guests to take as they please throughout the night so nobody will be going hungry.

We will have various activities to keep everyone busy throughout the relatively long reception such as Dancing, Bags, a Bar/Coffee Bar and more!
